About Dr. Sutapan Samanta

Dr. Sutapan Samanta is a Senior Fertility Consultant and IVF Specialist practicing in Kolkata, Asansol, and Durgapur in West Bengal. His prowess in the subject of Reproductive Medicine and Endocrinology has earned him a successful career in infertility management.

He has expertise in every aspect of Assisted Reproductive Therapy (ART – IUI, IVF, and ICSI), and follows evidence-based practices tailored to individual needs. With updated knowledge and excellent surgical skills, he has helped countless couples achieve their dream of parenthood. Currently, he is working as a Senior IVF Specialist with the expert panel of the Crysta IVF Centre in Kolkata.

His expertise includes Fertility Counselling, Affordable Individualised Stimulation Protocols, USG-guided Oocyte Retrieval and Embryo Transfer, Fertility Enhancing Surgeries (Hysteroscopy and Laparoscopy), and 3D/4D Ultrasound.
